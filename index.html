Dynamic Correlation

Usinig this dynamic plot you can discover correlations between different factors. For example: is there a correlation between "Household Income" and "Obesity". Interestingly, the answer to that question will be: "Yes, the lower the Household Income, the higher obesity level". Looking at the factors like Median Age and Smokers (%) we can conclude that most of the smokers in US are between 34 and 42 years old. I invite you to check out other factors and see what other findings this dynamic chart can reveal. The data set is based on 2014 ACS 1-year estimates from census.gov.
